How it works

One profile.
Every piece.

Built for the collector who treats their shelf like a portfolio.

01

Build your profile

Add your pieces, upload photos, and set each item to Display or Listed. Your public page is your reputation.

02

Get AI valuations

Instant market estimates pulled from real sold data — eBay, Goldin, PWCC, Heritage. No more guessing from comps.

03

Track your portfolio

See your total collection value, gains over time, and a diversification score across sports, athletes, and item types.

04

Add verification

Upload your PSA, JSA, or Beckett cert. Valued cross-checks it and locks a badge to your listing permanently.

05

Build provenance

Log ownership history — when you got it, from whom, for how much. Buyers inherit the full chain when they buy from you.

06

Sell direct

List your item on the marketplace. No auction house. Buyers see your verification, valuation history, and full profile.
